Welcome to my blog

Why I created this site and what I plan to write about here.

Welcome. I built this website as a place to document projects, collect ideas, and share what I learn.

The site is intentionally simple. Every post is an individual HTML file, which means I can write and publish without relying on a database, content-management system, or complicated build process.

What I plan to share

Posts may include project updates, useful resources, technical lessons, personal interests, and notes that I want to return to later.

This first entry is also a template for the general appearance of future posts. As the blog grows, I can continue refining the design without changing the basic publishing process.
